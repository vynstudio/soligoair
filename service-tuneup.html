Florida's climate runs your AC harder than almost anywhere in the country. Regular maintenance prevents expensive breakdowns, extends your system's life, and keeps energy bills in check.

What's included

Our comprehensive tune-up covers refrigerant levels, coil cleaning, electrical connections, capacitor testing, drain line clearing, thermostat calibration, airflow measurement, and a full system performance test.

📊

Why it matters

A well-maintained AC uses 15-20% less energy than a neglected one. Regular tune-ups also catch small problems before they become expensive emergencies — a $149 tune-up can prevent a $1,500 compressor failure.

📅

When to schedule

We recommend tune-ups twice a year in Florida: once in early spring before cooling season, and once in early fall after the summer demand. Air Care+ members get both included in their plan.

Air Care+ membership

For $29/month, Air Care+ includes two annual tune-ups, priority scheduling, 15% off all repairs, and no diagnostic fees. Most homeowners save more than the membership costs within the first year.
