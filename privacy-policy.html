Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Soligo Air (operated by PJWD Energy LLC) and describes how we collect, use, and protect information you share with us through soligoair.shop.

Information We Collect

When you request a quote, use our savings calculator, or contact us through the site, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Your service address or zip code
  • Details about your HVAC system or service need
  • Browser and device information collected automatically (via cookies and analytics tools)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you about your service request or quote
  • Schedule and dispatch HVAC service appointments
  • Follow up after a service visit
  • Send promotional offers or service reminders (you may opt out at any time)
  • Improve our website and customer experience

Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics and Meta Pixel to understand how visitors use the site and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. These tools may set cookies on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as our scheduling or CRM platforms) who assist us in operating the business, subject to confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
